Joe Burrows News
In Week 3, Joe Burrow needed to prove himself as a top QB in the league in order for Fantasy team
managers to keep him in their starting line up. Fortunately, the Bengals Quarterback was able to have
a rebound week, throwing three touchdowns against the New York Jets, with a total of 275 yards passing.
We hope Joe Burrow continues to perform as he did this past week in next weeks matchup against the
Miami Dolphins, who have a perfect record so far this season. Joe Burrow is healthy and expected to
play.
Ja'Marr Chase News
Ja'Marr Chase has had a steady peformance throughout the first three weeks of the NFL season. However,
he has slowed down from his first weeks performance from a whopping 129 yards receiving. Chase will need
to be targeted more if he wants to improve upon his week 2 and 3 performances as he has shown in the past
he can be a top 10 wide receiver in the league. Ja'Marr Chase is healthy and scheduled to play against the
Miami Dolphins for this weeks matchup.
